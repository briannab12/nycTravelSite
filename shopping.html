NYC is one of the best places to shop because of its diversity in stores. It is home to hundreds of stores, with a store for every style. You can find small gift shops and popular chain stores like Uniqlo, Old Navy, H&M, Express, Zara, LOFT, Victoria’s Secret, Banana Republic, Gap, American Eagle Outfitters, Nordstrom Rack, American Apparel, and Urban Outfitters. There are also a lot of designer stores such as Tiffany & Co., Armani, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Dolce & Gabbana, Kate Spade, and Prada. NYC has shopping malls and shopping districts, which are blocks lined with stores. Here are some of the best shopping districts and malls in NYC.

1. Union Square

Union Square is a great location in Manhattan to shop in chain stores. You will find Whole Foods, Forever 21, DSW, Nordstrom Rack, Barnes & Noble, Academy Records, Banana Republic, Victoria’s Secret, and Kenneth Cole.

2. The Shops at Columbus Circle

The Shops at Columbus Circle mall have some of the nicest and most expensive stores. While shopping, you also have a great view of central park. Coach, Diesel, H&M, J. Crew, Michael Kors, and Swarovski are some of the stores you will find here.

3. SoHo

SoHo is known for being one of the most fashionable neighborhoods in NYC. SoHo is in Lower Manhattan and has cobblestone streets and loft apartments. Some of the stores at SoHo are J. Crew, Old Navy, Club Monaco, Bebe, Victoria's Secret, H&M, Bloomingdale’s, Prada, and Uniqlo.

4. Herald Square

Herald Square & the Garment District is known for being the home of Macy’s, but there are many more stores in this shopping district. Aerospatiale, Express, American Eagle, Gap, H&M, Foot Locker, Sephora, Uniqlo, Urban Outfitters, Zara, and Lush are some of the many stores found in this area.

5. Fifth Avenue & 57th Street

Fifth Avenue & 57th Street is a luxury shopping district. It is home to many designer stores such as Tiffany & Co., Louis Vuitton, Chanel Dior, Versace, and Cartier. Come prepared to spend a small fortune.
