Food you have to try

https://www.pexels.com/photo/food-chicken-meat-outdoors-8572/

New York City (NYC) has so many places to eat that you could eat at a new restaurant every day for 21 years and not run out of new places to try. More importantly, unlike most college towns, NYC has restaurants from a magnitude of cultures that offer dishes authentic to that culture that have not been Americanized. You can find Mexican, Spanish, Japanese, Greek, Mediterranean, French, Philippine, Pakistani, Chinese, Ethiopian, Jamaican, Taiwanese, and many other cuisines. This makes NYC any foodies dream location to visit. NYC also offers a plethora of dining experiences for any budget that do not sacrifice taste. Do not be afraid to stop at a food truck or a restaurant the size of a walk-in closet, because in NYC you cannot judge the quality of food by the location it is served. So, whether you are looking for a cheap eat or a royalty-grade dining experience, you can find a restaurant with five-star food. Here are a few restaurants that come highly recommended.

1. Shake Shack

Shake Shack is a NYC classic. They have great simple burgers and fries and are a cheap-eat. Plus, while you are eating your burger, you can enjoy Madison Square Park.

2. Roberta's

What is NYC without pizza? Roberta’s is known for their brick-oven pizza, but they also have other items on the menu. Plus, they deliver!

3. Red Rooster

Red Rooster has modern décor and is a great place to have fun. Eat, drink, dance, and enjoy yourself!

4. Smorgasburg

Smorgasburg should be every foodies destination. The street is filled with food vendors on Saturday from 10-5 with plenty of cheap-eat options. Why pick one restaurant? Eat a little bit of everything till you can’t eat anymore. They are sure to have something you will like.

5. Peter Luger Steak House

Although they try, no stake house in NYC compares to Peter Luger Steak House. It is pricey, but every bite will be cherished. Dine here to enjoy the best steak in NYC.
